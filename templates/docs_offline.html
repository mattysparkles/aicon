AICon Offline Setup

Use this guide when web access is unavailable.

Phone Tree (Onboarding Number)

  1. Call the onboarding number and say: “signup”.
  2. Speak your full name.
  3. Speak your prison ID.
  4. If you have an affiliate code, speak it; or say “none”.
  5. To set a security phrase, say: “set pass” and speak a phrase.
  6. To pay by phone, say: “pay”. Follow the prompts to enter card details.

SMS Setup (Onboarding Number)

  1. Text “signup”.
  2. Reply with your full name.
  3. Reply with your prison ID.
  4. Reply with your affiliate code or “none”.
  5. Optional: Text “SET PASS <your phrase>”.
  6. To pay by link, text “pay pro” (or “pay basic”).

After Activation

You will receive your personal AI number to call or text directly.
